BONSAI-U

Join our online bonsai learning platform, Bonsai-U, and get access to bonsai related videos and live Q&A sessions each week. Bonsai-U provides insight for beginners to advanced enthusiasts, covering horticultural, design and maintenance aspects of bonsai art.

INTENSIVE BONSAI SCHOOL

Our Intensive Bonsai School provides hands-on learning opportunities for those who are serious about taking their skills to the next level. We are currently in the process of relocating to Kyoto, Japan, so we are unable to take on new students at this time. However, if you are interested in studying at Eisei-en Kyoto in the future, please feel free to join the waiting list below!

JAPAN BONSAI TOURS

Want to experience true bonsai art in Japan? Join one of our Japan Bonsai Tours and travel with us to various bonsai gardens, nurseries and exhibitions. Each tour is designed to provide participants insight into Japanese culture, cuisine and traditional arts, from bonsai to macha tea to shintoism. Join us for an experience of a lifetime!
